Our Story

The Magic Behind What-A-Toy!

At What-A-Toy!, we believe that toys are more than just objects — they are the building blocks of imagination, creativity, and lifelong memories.

Our Story

Wonder in Every Corner

Our mission is to bring the joy of play back to its roots: the physical experience. With 5 premier locations across Oklahoma, Texas, Kansas, and South Dakota, we have created a haven where children and families can explore, touch, and discover the latest trends alongside timeless classics.

We don’t just sell toys; we curate experiences that spark curiosity and bring smiles to every face. Step into any of our stores to find a world-class selection of high-quality toys, expert advice from our “Play Enthusiasts,” and an atmosphere where wonder has no limits.

Our Play Enthusiasts

Staff Who Actually Play

Our store team doesn’t just ring up sales — they demo kits, test puzzles, and know every character by name. Ask about a toy and you’ll get a story, a recommendation, and usually a grin.
