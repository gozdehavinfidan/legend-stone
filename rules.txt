# Elegant Stone - Project Rules

## 1. Project Purpose
This project is a promotional and brand presentation website for **Elegant Stone**, a mall store with a premium and culturally rich product range.

This website is **NOT an e-commerce website at this stage**.
Its main purpose is to:
- introduce the Elegant Stone brand
- showcase product categories
- reflect the store’s atmosphere and aesthetic identity
- encourage users to visit the physical store or contact the brand

The website should be structured in a way that allows future expansion into:
- online catalog
- multilingual support
- e-commerce
- admin-managed content updates

---

## 2. Brand Identity
**Brand name:** Elegant Stone

The website must reflect the following brand qualities:
- premium
- elegant
- warm
- ethnic
- spiritual
- artistic
- culturally rich
- modern but authentic

The Elegant Stone brand should feel like a curated boutique that brings together decorative beauty, natural materials, artisan aesthetics, and cultural atmosphere.

The website must NOT feel:
- cheap
- generic
- overly corporate
- visually noisy
- cluttered
- marketplace-like

---

## 3. Product Scope
Elegant Stone includes product categories such as:
- natural and polished gemstones
- jewelry
- bracelets
- rings
- necklaces
- ethnic clothing
- home decor
- Turkish mosaic lamps
- spiritual products
- incense
- natural oils and fragrances
- swords and knives
- gifts and decorative objects
- wall decor

Products are presented for showcase and brand storytelling purposes only.
No direct shopping flow should be implemented at this stage.

---

## 4. Required Pages
The website should include at minimum:
- Home
- About
- Categories
- Gallery
- Visit Us / Store Info / The Experience
- Contact

Optional future pages:
- Blog
- Collection pages
- Product details
- Online catalog

---

## 5. Design Rules
- The design should feel luxurious, immersive, elegant, and warm.
- Dark mode is the primary visual identity.
- Light mode should also be supported.
- Use a refined visual hierarchy.
- Use generous spacing and clean layouts.
- Avoid visual clutter.
- Use subtle and smooth animations only.
- Avoid excessive motion effects.
- Avoid overly bright or childish colors.

Suggested palette direction:
- charcoal black
- dark brown
- deep olive / emerald
- warm gold
- copper
- amber
- optional burgundy accents

---

## 6. Typography Rules
- Headings should feel elegant and premium.
- Body text must be highly readable.
- Serif or display-style headings may be combined with clean sans-serif body text.
- Typography should support the premium and cultural tone of Elegant Stone.

---

## 7. Content Rules
- Tone of voice should be warm, elegant, refined, and brand-oriented.
- Content must not sound too aggressive, salesy, or generic.
- Category descriptions should be atmospheric and inviting.
- Text should highlight craftsmanship, authenticity, cultural richness, and aesthetics.
- No pricing, stock, checkout, or cart-related language should appear in the current version.
- The Elegant Stone brand voice should feel premium, artistic, and welcoming.
- Avoid direct medical, therapeutic, or guaranteed spiritual outcome claims.
- Spiritual language may be used in a lifestyle and atmospheric sense, but should remain soft and inspirational.

## 11. UX Rules
- Navigation must be simple and intuitive.
- Users should easily explore categories.
- Important actions should include:
  - explore categories
  - view gallery
  - visit store
  - contact Elegant Stone
- The homepage should not be overcrowded.
- The site should prioritize clarity, atmosphere, and discoverability.

---

## 12. Technical Rules
- Use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
- Code must be clean, readable, modular, and reusable.
- Use consistent naming conventions.
- Use CSS variables for colors, spacing, and theme management.
- Structure components clearly (navbar, hero, category cards, gallery, contact, footer).
- Avoid unnecessary heavy libraries unless there is a clear reason.

---

## 13. Responsive Rules
- The site must be fully responsive.
- Mobile-first thinking is encouraged.
- Layouts must work well on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
- Navigation must remain usable on smaller screens.
- Buttons and interactive elements must be touch-friendly.

---

## 14. Accessibility Rules
- Use semantic HTML where possible.
- Maintain good contrast ratios.
- Ensure text readability.
- Add alt text support for images.
- Keep interactive elements keyboard-friendly when possible.

---

## 15. Performance Rules
- Optimize image usage.
- Avoid excessive animation or large scripts.
- Keep load times reasonable.
- Prefer lightweight solutions.

---

## 16. Future Scalability Rules
- The structure should allow future addition of:
  - product detail pages
  - e-commerce features
  - multilingual support
  - CMS or admin panel integration
- New categories and content sections should be easy to add.

---

## 17. Prohibited Features for Current Phase
Do NOT add:
- cart
- checkout
- payment system
- account system
- stock tracking
- product pricing logic
- marketplace-style dense product grids

This phase is strictly for brand presentation and category showcase.

---

## 18. Visual Experience Goal
The final Elegant Stone website should feel like a premium digital showroom for a unique boutique brand that combines:
- ethnic culture
- spirituality
- decorative beauty
- artisan aesthetics
- modern luxury
